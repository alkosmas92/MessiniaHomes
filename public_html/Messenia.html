MESSENIA

Largely untouched by mass tourism, Messenia is home to cultural values, traditions, and a history of over 4,500 years, living in harmony with one of the stunning landscapes of the Mediterranean. With a history from the first civilizations, from the archaic times to the ancient years, followed the medieval days with Byzantium and the Venetian domination one of the first regions that joined the new Greek state. Many Historic places to visit, such as the palace of Nestor in the village of Chora, the ancient Messina in the center of the area with stunning views and of course the beautiful Venetian castles of Koroni, Methoni and Pylos.

HISTORY

With a history from the first civilizations, from the archaic times to the ancient years, followed the medieval days with Byzantium and the Venetian domination of one of the first states that are independent in the new Greek state. Many historical places to visit, such as the palace of Nestor in the village of Chora, the ancient Messina in the center of the area with stunning views and of course the beautiful Venetian castles of Koroni, Methoni and Pylos.

EARTH

The olive oil produced for the Koroneiki olive variety, is one of the best olive oils in the world, awarded several times in many competitions. Kalamata, the capital of Messenia, is synonymous with black olives.

MOVEMENTS

Messenia has an expanded provincial and national road network and can be reached very easily from Athens with a fast and safe highway. There is also an international airport and during the March-October period, there are many international flights.

SPORTS AND CONNECTIVITY

If you enjoy sports, almost everything is available: 3 beautiful golf courses - and many more, tennis courts, mountain biking and jogging areas. Internet and 4G coverage is almost 100%.
